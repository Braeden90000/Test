Create your custom PFP

The Discord Avatar Maker lets you create your personal customized avatar, perfect to use as a profile picture or server logo in the Discord app. Simply choose out of hundreds of possible shape combinations, set the color to your liking, and hit the download button! The recommended download specs for your pfp / logo are 512x512px PNG, but download with smaller or bigger dimensions and SVG export are also possible.

Make an animated GIF

With the animation tool you can create an animated GIF out of your avatar. These can be used with a Nitro subscription or if your Server is boosted enough. The GIF will be generated in the browser, so it may take a while once you hit the download button.

About

The Maker is specifically tailored for your pfp or server logo in Discord. If your operating system allows it, they can even be used to replace the default Discord app icon.
Please do not use the graphics generated here to represent the Discord brand, as this collides with their brand guidelines.

Disclaimer: If you are on iOS, please make sure to use the Safari browser, as you may experience issues with the download otherwise. Sorry!
